About Me

Joel Fabok

Hi, my name is Joel and for the past five years, I have been honing my skills in designing and creating exceptional websites. I am proud to have earned a certificate in Full Stacks Development through York University, where I acquired valuable knowledge and expertise in the field. My passion lies in helping non-profit organizations and businesses of all sizes to effectively market their products and services online. I specialize in updating, maintaining, and redesigning websites and social media pages to ensure that they are not only aesthetically pleasing but also highly functional and optimized for maximum impact. If you're interested in seeing some of my previous work, please feel free to visit the portfolio section of my website. I look forward to working with you and helping your brand shine in the digital world!
